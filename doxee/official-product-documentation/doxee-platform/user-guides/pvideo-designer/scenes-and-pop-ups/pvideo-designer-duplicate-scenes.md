To duplicate an existing scene or pop-up:

- **Navigate to the Projects tab and open a Project**. This action automatically opens the Storyboard editor, and the first Storyboard in the Project is displayed.

- **In the left sidebar, **select the scene or pop-up you want to duplicate.

- **Open the** **three-dot menu** of the scene or pop-up and click **Duplicate**.

- **Confirm the action**.

The selected scene or pop-up will be duplicated and added to the left sidebar.
