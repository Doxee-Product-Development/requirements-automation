The **Platform Analytics** toggle enables event tracking of video views in Babelee for monitoring purposes. By default, video views remain anonymous, and no tracking cookies are used.

To activate or deactivate Analytics tracking, open the **Project settings** &gt; **Advanced** and scroll to the section **Analytics tracking**. Click the **Platform Analytics toggle** to activate or deactivate it.
