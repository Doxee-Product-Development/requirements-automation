A thumbnail is a static image used to represent the entire video. It serves as a visual aid to easily recognize a specific video and get an idea of its contents.

By default, the system uses the first frame of the video as the thumbnail image. To set a different thumbnail image:

- Open the **Project settings** &gt; **General** and navigate to the section **Thumbnail**.

- Click the **Upload new** button and select an image from your device (up to 500KB).
