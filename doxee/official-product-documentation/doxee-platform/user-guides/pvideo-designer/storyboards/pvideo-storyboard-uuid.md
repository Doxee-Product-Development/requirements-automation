The UUID is the unique ID of the Storyboard and takes the form of an immutable alphanumeric string, for example, `90052299-a1c4-4d79-ae00-830b5cc05597`. The UUID may be used in various configurations, for example, when indicating the specific Storyboard to use for the final interactive video.

To copy the UUID:

- **Navigate to the Projects tab and open a Project**. This action automatically opens the Storyboard editor, and the first Storyboard in the Project is displayed.

- **Open the Storyboard selector** and select the Storyboard you want to copy.

- **Open the** **three-dot menu** and click **Copy UUID**.
