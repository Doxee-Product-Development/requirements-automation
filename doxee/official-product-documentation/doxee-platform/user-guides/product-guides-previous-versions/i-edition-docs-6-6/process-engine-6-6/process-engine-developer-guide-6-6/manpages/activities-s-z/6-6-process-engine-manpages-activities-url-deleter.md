(urlDeleter, Module: [Base](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-detailed-module-descriptions-base)) 

Deletes resources.

**Input Parameters:**

- urls (x-infinica/url) (1-unbounded)

The URL or URLs specifying the resource(s) to delete.

- recursive (x-xsd/boolean)

Flag to specify if directories should be removed recursively.

Default:

true
