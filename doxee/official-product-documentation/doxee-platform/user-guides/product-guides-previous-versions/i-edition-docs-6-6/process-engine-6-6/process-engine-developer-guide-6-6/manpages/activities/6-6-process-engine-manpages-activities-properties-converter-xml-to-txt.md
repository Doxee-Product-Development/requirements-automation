(propertiesXml2Txt, Module: [Base](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-detailed-module-descriptions-base))

Reads properties from an xml source and returns their plain text representation.

**Input Parameter:**

- xmlProperties (x-infinica/stream) (1-unbounded) 

The xml representation of the properties.

**Output Parameter:**

- txtProperties (x-infinica/stream) (1-unbounded)

The plain text representation of the properties, suitable for a *.properties file.
