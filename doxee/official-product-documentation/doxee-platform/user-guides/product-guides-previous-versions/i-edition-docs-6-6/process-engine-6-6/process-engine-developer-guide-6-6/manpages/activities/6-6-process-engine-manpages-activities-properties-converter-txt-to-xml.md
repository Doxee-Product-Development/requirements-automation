(propertiesTxt2Xml, Module: [Base](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-detailed-module-descriptions-base))

Reads properties from a plain text source and returns their xml representation.

**Input Parameter:**

- txtProperties (x-infinica/stream) (1-unbounded)

The plain text representation of the properties, most commonly from a *.properties file.

**Output Parameter:**

- xmlProperties (x-infinica/stream) (1-unbounded) 

The xml representation of the properties.
