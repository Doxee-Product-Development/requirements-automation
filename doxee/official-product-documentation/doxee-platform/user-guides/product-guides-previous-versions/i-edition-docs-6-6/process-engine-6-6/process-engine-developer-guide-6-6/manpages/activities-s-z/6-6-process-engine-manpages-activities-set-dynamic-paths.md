(setDynamicPaths, Module: [Core](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-detailed-module-descriptions-core))

Maps names to paths, overriding any path definitions that may be active in the project.

**Input Parameters:**

- pathNames (text/plain) (1-unbounded) 

Names for the mapped paths.

- pathTargets (x-infinica/url) (1-unbounded)

Targets for the mapped paths.
