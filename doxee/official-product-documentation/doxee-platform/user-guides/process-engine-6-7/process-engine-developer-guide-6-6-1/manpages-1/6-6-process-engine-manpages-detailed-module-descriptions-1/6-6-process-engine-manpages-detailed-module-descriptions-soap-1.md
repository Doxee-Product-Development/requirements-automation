Support for calling SOAP services.

Activities:	

[SOAP Caller](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-activities-soap-caller-1), [Web Service Caller](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-activities-web-service-caller-1)

The soapCaller activity supports SOAP 1.1 and 1.2 over the HTTP (or HTTPS) protocol. If the SOAP service requires authentication, a matching preemptive entry must be made available in the credentials registry.
