(resetRetryCounter, Module: [Core](/doxee-platform/docs/6-6-process-engine-manpages-detailed-module-descriptions-core-1)) 

Resets the retry counter of an exception connection.

**Input Parameter:**

- connectionId (text/plain)

Exception connection ID. Must either be a full ID or begin with a '.' character, in which case the ID is treated as relative to the current context.
