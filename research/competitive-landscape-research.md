### 1.3 Products and Platform

#### Doxee Platform (core product)

The Doxee Platform is a cloud-native, AI-powered CCM/CXM platform combining six functional scopes plus AI Foundation:

**DESIGN** - Content Creation and Modeling
- **Data Designer**: Visual low-code ETL for data transformation (CSV, XML, Flat Files, TXT, PDF, AFP)
- **Data Transformation (DT3)**: Advanced data mapping with 122 documented operators
- **Template Designer**: AI-enhanced template creation supporting PDF, PDF/A, PDF/UA, PostScript, AFP, Word, HTML, Text, XSL-FO
- **Business Designer**: Visual business rules, decision tables, conditional logic
- **Video Designer**: Interactive video template authoring (Pvideo)
- **Process Designer**: BPMN 2.0 workflow orchestration
- **Batch Workflows**: High-volume batch document production
- **On-Demand Workflows**: Real-time event-triggered document generation
- **Output Management**: Multi-channel delivery configuration

**ENGAGEMENT** - Interactive Experiences
- **Interactive Documents (Workplace)**: Dynamic document workspace for business users
- **Composer**: Ad-hoc document composition interface
- **Pweb (Personalized Forms & Microsites)**: Web-based interactive forms and microsites
- **Pvideo (Interactive Videos)**: Personalized data-driven video experiences with User-Directed Storytelling (UDS)
- **Digital Archive**: Long-term compliant document storage and retrieval

**INSIGHTS** - Analytics and Intelligence
- **Customer Insights**: Customer engagement metrics
- **Process Insights**: Workflow performance analytics
- **Tracking & Reporting**: Communication delivery and engagement tracking
- **Channel Insights**: Cross-channel performance dashboards

**INTEGRATION** - System Connectivity
- **Marketplace Apps**: Vendor-certified apps for Salesforce, SAP, Microsoft Dynamics 365 (on respective marketplaces)
- **APIs and Connectors**: REST APIs, pre-built connectors, open standard support (XML, XSLT, XSL-FO, BPMN)

**AUTOMATION** - Process Execution
- **Process Engine**: BPMN 2.0 engine with elastic scalability
- **Omnichannel Engine**: Unified channel routing (print, email, SMS, PEC, SERC, SERCQ, AppIO, web, video)
- **Batch Jobs / On-Demand Jobs**: Legacy and modern job execution

**ADMINISTRATION** - Platform Management
- **Command Center**: Centralized monitoring
- **Repository / Resource Explorer**: Content lifecycle management
- **User Manager / Settings**: RBAC, tenant configuration
- **Doxee University**: Self-service e-learning portal

**AI FOUNDATION** (cross-platform AI layer)
- Template & Content Generation (GenAI for text creation, summarization, rephrasing)
- Template Migration (AI-assisted legacy migration)
- Composer Copilot (AI authoring assistance)
- Engagement Scoring (predictive analytics)
- Customer Data Hub (roadmap)

- **Sentiment Analysis**: AI-generated sentiment scores flow into journey maps
- **PII Detection**: Directly in authoring experience to prevent accidental data exposure
- **AI Scripting Assistant**: Pre-trained assistant writes/reviews/compares production scripts from prompts
- **Automated Translation**: On-demand or bulk; spell check in 150+ languages
- **Content Intelligence**: Repository-wide analysis dashboard for content performance, channel response data

#### Pricing Model
- **Inspire Flex**: Enterprise licensing (perpetual or subscription) + maintenance
- **Inspire Evolve**: SaaS subscription (per-user or per-transaction)

- Pricing is not publicly listed; RFP-based

#### Key Strengths
- Most comprehensive AI capabilities in CCM (QKS MVP designation)

- AnyPrem deployment flexibility

- Open APIs, prebuilt Salesforce/Adobe Analytics/Dynamics plugins
- Massive enterprise installed base (banking, insurance, healthcare, government)

#### Key Weaknesses
- AI-driven content analysis and optimization still evolving (per QKS)
- Real-time analytics focus primarily on operational efficiency; deeper behavioral analytics may need external platforms
- Platform complexity for smaller organizations
- High total cost of ownership for enterprise deployments

| Phase | Era | Focus |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **CCM 1.0** (pre-2015) | Document Generation | Batch printing, compliance output |
| **CCM 2.0** (2015-2020) | Digital Communications | Omnichannel delivery, digital-first |
| **CCM 3.0 / CXM** (2020-2025) | Experience Management | Journey orchestration, personalization, analytics |
| **CXM 2.0 / Agentic** (2025+) | AI-Orchestrated Experiences | Agentic AI, autonomous communication agents, self-optimizing |

Per QKS Group (2025), by 2026 roughly 50% of new communication processes will include AI-driven content generation or decision support. By 2027, the first wave of autonomous "communication agents" will manage entire messaging sequences. By 2028, CCM platforms become self-optimizing and self-healing CXM platforms.

## 6. Strategic Implications

### 6.1 Doxee's Competitive Position Summary

**Where Doxee Leads:**
- Personalized interactive video (Pvideo with UDS) -- unique in CCM market
- Interactive microsites (Pweb) -- no direct equivalent
- European data sovereignty and GDPR-native architecture
- Sustainability measurement (Hyperion) and B Corp status
- Italian compliance ecosystem (e-invoicing, PEC/SERC, digital archiving)
- Cloud-native any-prem flexibility
- BPMN 2.0 open-standard workflow engine
- Advanced data transformation (DT3)

**Where Doxee is Competitive:**
- Template design and composition (strong across print and digital)
- Omnichannel delivery (broad channel coverage including trusted channels)
- CRM/ERP integration (Salesforce, SAP, Dynamics 365 marketplace apps)
- Batch and on-demand processing
- AI content generation (emerging)

**Where Doxee Must Close Gaps:**
- Journey mapping and orchestration (no production capability today; Quadient leads by 2+ years)
- Content rationalization (no template estate analysis/consolidation; Messagepoint and Quadient lead)
- AI authoring depth (Composer Copilot is early; Quadient has production in-editor AI suite)
- Multilingual translation at scale (Quadient: 150+ languages; Messagepoint: MARCIE)
- Headless CCM / API-first content distribution (Messagepoint leads)
- BYOAI model governance (Quadient only vendor with this)
- Geographic expansion beyond Europe (heavily concentrated in CEE/Italy)

### 6.2 Path from "Scaling for Impact" to "Industry Pioneer"

Based on QKS Group AI Maturity Matrix criteria, Doxee must demonstrate:

1. **AI-First Productization**: Deepen AI across the entire content lifecycle (not just generation) -- authoring, rationalization, sentiment, PII, translation, testing/evaluation
2. **AI Vision & Roadmap**: Articulate and execute a clear 3-5 year plan for AI-orchestrated communication experiences, including agentic capabilities
3. **Operational Hardening**: Prove enterprise scale with measurable outcomes, real-time orchestration, event triggers, channel optimization at high volumes
4. **Journey Integration**: Connect CCM to customer journey programs with native journey mapping and analytics
